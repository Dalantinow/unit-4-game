CRYSTALS COLLECTOR

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals, and by clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your score.

You win by matching your total score to the randomly generated number, and you lose if your total goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game should change the individual crystal values.
